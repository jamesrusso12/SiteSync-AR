The Process

From Zero to Site Intelligence in 3 Steps

SiteIQ is designed for the job site, not the boardroom. No training required.

1

Scan

Point your iPhone 16 Pro at the site. The LiDAR sensor builds a precise 3D mesh of the terrain in real time — walls, floors, slopes, and all. The more you scan, the more accurate your data.

2

Place

Two taps drop your building footprint onto the real terrain. Tap the corner, tap the front edge — SiteIQ does the rest. GPS and compass align your BIM model to true north at 1:1 scale.

3

Know

See instant cut and fill cubic yardage, or walk your Revit/Rhino model on the actual site to spot clashes before they become $20,000 change orders. Share findings directly from the field.

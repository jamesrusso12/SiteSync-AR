Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

SiteIQ ("we", "us") is an early-stage product built in Boise, Idaho. This policy explains what we collect through this website and how we use it. We keep it short because we collect very little.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you submit it through our waitlist form. That's the only personal data this site asks for.
  • Basic request logs — like any website, our host (GitHub Pages) may log IP addresses and browser user-agents for security and abuse prevention. We do not control or retain these beyond the host's defaults.

We do not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics, or cookies on this site.

How the waitlist works

Waitlist submissions are processed by Formspree, which forwards your email address to us. Your address is subject to Formspree's privacy policy in transit. We use it only to contact you about SiteIQ early access — nothing else.

How we use your email

  • To notify you when early access opens.
  • To occasionally update you on product progress relevant to the waitlist.

We will not sell, rent, or share your email with unrelated third parties.

Your choices

You can ask us to remove your email at any time — just email hello@siteiq.app and we'll delete it. Every message we send will also include an unsubscribe option.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@siteiq.app.
